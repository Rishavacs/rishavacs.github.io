Our Curriculum

Inspiring Education, Shaping Futures

At Sharda Gyan Niketan, our curriculum is thoughtfully aligned with the guidelines prescribed by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. We focus on building a strong academic foundation while encouraging critical thinking, creativity, values, and holistic development.

Foundational Stage (Nursery - Grade 2)

Play-based, activity-driven learning to develop communication, motor, emotional, and social skills in a joyful environment.

Preparatory Stage (Grade 3 - 5)

Focus on language, numeracy, environmental studies, and creativity. Encouraging curiosity and foundational concepts.

Middle Stage (Grade 6 - 8)

Emphasis on critical thinking, subject depth, project work, and foundational coding and science experiments.

Secondary Stage (Grade 9 - 12)

Disciplined subject learning, career exposure, practicals, mentorship, and preparation for competitive exams and board success.

Co-curricular Integration

Music, arts, sports, yoga, dance, and club activities are embedded across stages to ensure physical, emotional, and creative growth.

Skill-Based Learning

Financial literacy, entrepreneurship, digital tools, leadership training and environmental awareness woven into academics.
